About Us

Our History

Selsey Cricket Club was founded in 2010 to bring together local cricketers and residents of Crawley and Broadfield. Our home cricket ground is located at Goffs Park in Crawley. We play an attractive list of friendly matches against both local clubs and wandering sides from inner London.

Membership Options

At Selsey CC we have different levels of membership on offer. There is membership to cover playing cricket this automatically allows use of the club on a social basis during our membership year (April-March). Alternatively if you are only looking for a social membership these are available too.

Fees and Schedule

The annual fee for junior members include winter nets, T20 match fees and membership of Selsey CC (a compulsory membership fee of £13 for junior playing members).
The annual fee for senior members include a compulsory membership of £26 for senior playing members.
Festivals are not included.
Family Discount – A discount of 20% off membership fees is available to families with three or more members (one of which must be an adult member).
